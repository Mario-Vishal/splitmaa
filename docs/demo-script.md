# Demo Script

1. Open the app in Expo.
2. Show Home metrics and local persistence status.
3. Open the assistant.
4. Run: `Add 8 dollars for milk paid by me using credit card split with Alex`.
5. Show the confirmation card and execution plan.
6. Confirm the action.
7. Show updated Home metrics, Groups feed, Contacts balance, and Diagnostics audit log.
8. Run: `How much does Alex owe me?`.
9. Show the grounded answer from local data.
10. Run: `Write me a poem`.
11. Show unsupported request handling.
